Smith & Wesson M&P 15-22 Sport .22 LR Semiautomatic Rifle
The Smith & Wesson® M&P 15-22 Sport .22 LR Semiautomatic Rimfire Rifle is a true M&P 15-style rifle built from the ground up as a dedicated .22 LR semiautomatic – not an awkward conversion. Features a 10" M&P Slim Handguard with Magpul® M-Lok®, Magpul folding MBUS® sights, six-position telescoping stock (compliant model comes with a fixed stock) and 2" M-Lok Rail Panel that delivers customization options for optics and accessories. Two-position receiver-mounted safety lever, functioning charging handles.
